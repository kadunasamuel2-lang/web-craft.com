FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with us.

How long does it take to build a website?+
Most websites are completed in 1–3 weeks. A simple business site takes 5–7 days; a full e-commerce store may take 2–4 weeks. We agree on a timeline before we start.
How much does a website cost in Nigeria?+
A basic business website starts from ₦80,000. E-commerce stores and complex projects are priced higher. WhatsApp us for a free custom quote.
What types of websites do you build?+
All types: e-commerce stores, church websites, solar company websites, beauty/makeup stores, corporate sites, NGO websites, food/restaurant sites, and more.
Do you offer post-launch maintenance?+
Yes! Monthly plans keep your site updated, secure and running smoothly — updates, backups, security checks and minor edits included.
Can you rank my business on Google in Nigeria?+
Absolutely. We specialise in Nigeria-focused SEO and have helped many businesses reach Google's first page within weeks of launch.
What do I need to provide to get started?+
Just reach out! We need your business details, preferred style, any existing logo/content, and your goals. We guide you through everything else.
